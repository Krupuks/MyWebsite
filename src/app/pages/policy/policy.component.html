Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2023-10-17
This Privacy Policy elucidates the practices of Kevin Van Rooy concerning the acquisition and utilization of information when users interact with the portfolio website of Kevin Van Rooy at 'https://kevinvanrooy.be'. This policy delineates your privacy rights and the legal safeguards afforded by privacy laws.

Information Collected
When users browse our portfolio website, we collect Usage Data. This encompasses various types of information related to user interactions with the website, allowing us to gain insights into user behavior and preferences.

Data Collection Method
Our data collection occurs passively as users navigate through the website. This includes the use of cookies and similar technologies, as well as server logs, which enable us to gather Usage Data.

Data Retention
We will retain the collected information for a period of 1 year and 1 month. This duration allows us to analyze trends, enhance user experience, and maintain a record of website activity.

Use of Collected Information
The information we collect is employed for the provision and upkeep of our Service. This encompasses tasks such as website optimization, content customization, and troubleshooting to ensure an optimal user experience.

Sharing of Collected Information
We will only share collected information with user consent. This may occur in cases where users choose to interact with third-party services integrated into our website.

Third Party Cookies
Our website utilizes third-party cookies, specifically from Google Analytics, for the purpose of analyzing website performance and user behavior. These cookies facilitate the generation of statistical data used to optimize the website's functionality.

Cookie Categories
The cookies employed on our website fall into two main categories: Strictly necessary cookies, which are essential for basic website functionality, and Performance cookies, which help us analyze user interactions to improve the Service.
